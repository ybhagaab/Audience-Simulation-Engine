======================================================================
KEY FINDING: ML Tag Over-Prediction
======================================================================

The ML persona model assigns 95-100% of users to nearly every interest
category, making the tags undifferentiated and commercially useless for
targeting. Amazon-sourced tags (actual shopping behavior) show much more
realistic and differentiated rates:

  Category        ML Rate    Amazon Rate    ML Over-Prediction
  Travel          95%        50-79%         +16 to +44 points
  Parents/Baby    92%        54-80%         +12 to +38 points
  Automotive      97%        63-84%         +10 to +34 points
  Home/Kitchen    99%        69-88%         +11 to +30 points

For the Male Crossover and Engaged Explorer personas (75% Amazon-only
users), the ML model severely UNDER-predicts because it has no data
on these users. Amazon tags are the only reliable commercial signal
for these segments.

Recommendation: Use Amazon-sourced rates as the primary commercial
signal. Flag ML-only profiles as "predicted — lower confidence."
